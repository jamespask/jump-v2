About

Experience the highs and lows of competitive showjumping in our captivating behind-the-scenes documentary series. A sport in which there is no discrimination, where men and women compete alongside each other at the same events, at the same heights and under the same rules. The emphasis is on skill and viewers watch as rider and horse conquer obstacles and face defeat.

The series will offer an in-depth and unfiltered look at the preparation, training and dedication required to excel in this intensive, exhilarating equestrian sport. It will capture the atmosphere, rivalry and camaraderie that define the showjumping circuit which, in recent years, has seen an influx of celebrity daughters.

With the spotlight on Jamie Gornall's pursuit of the 2024 Paris Olympics, viewers will follow him on his quest to represent Great Britain and will witness his emotional journey as he competes in various qualifying events and strives to achieve peak performance. Jamie's story will serve as a source of inspiration and motivation.

The series will also follow a diverse group of talented riders as they navigate the challenges of the show jumping circuit. It will delve into their personal stories exploring their triumphs, setbacks and the sacrifices they make in pursuit of their dreams.

Meet Jamie

Born in Blackburn, England to working class parents, Jamie defied expectations. From football prodigy to conquering difficult horses, he embraced every challenge. A single riding lesson propelled him into the ranks of Di Lampard, head coach of the British team. With determination, he turned pro at 21, keeping his promise to finish his studies.

When Olympic medalist Rolf-Goran Bengtsson came calling at 24, Jamie moved to Hamburg, risking it all, even the ire of the British federation for riding alongside Janne Friederike Meyer. At 27, a devastating knee injury threatened his dreams, but through surgeries and relentless rehabilitation, he made an inspiring comeback. Joining forces with the world's greatest showjumper, Ludger Bearbaum, Jamie soared to new heights. As the Tokyo Olympics beckoned, fate dealt a blow with a sold horse and the pandemic, but Jamie remains undeterred, setting his sights on Paris 2024.

Partner with JUMP

The alignment between the elegance and high performance of equestrian sports along with the adventure and spirit of exploration of showjumping makes for a natural fit with brands that want to showcase their values and associate with these qualities.
