Episodes

The central thread through all episodes is Jamie Gornall's journey towards Olympic selection. Each episode will explore different aspects of the sport providing an in-depth and comprehensive look at the intricacies and challenges faced by those involved. From the selection and training of horses to the intense preparation of riders, viewers will witness the dedication, passion and resilience required to excel in showjumping. The series will also highlight the global nature of showjumping, from the sprawling showgrounds of North America to the historic arenas of Europe. Capturing the atmosphere, rivalry and camaraderie that define the showjumping circuit. One episode will shed light on the various types of sponsorship, ranging from individual rider endorsements to corporate partnerships with events or equestrian brands. Interviews with riders, sponsors and industry experts could provide insights into the criteria sponsors look for when selecting riders or events to support. Discussing the financial support would highlight the impact sponsors have on the competitive showjumping community and offer valuable lessons and inspiration for aspiring show jumpers.

01 | AMERICAN DREAMS

This episode introduces Jamie Gornall and showcases his bold decision to move to America in pursuit of his showjumping career. The episode captures the excitement, the uncertainty and anticipation as Jamie embraces a new environment. Viewers witness the adjustments he must make from building new connections and partnerships to adapting to different training methods and competition formats.

02 | SHOWJUMPING IS HARD

Training showjumping horses requires patience, consistency and a deep understanding of equine behaviour with a focus on building a strong foundation and gradually introducing new challenges. Role of coach - more than a trainer, they are a mentor, strategist and support system for both the rider and horse. A winning team requires a strong coach. Exclusive access to the stables and courses. A behindthe scenes look at the lives of riders and their horses as they train, compete and overcome obstaces in the thrilling sport of showjumping.

03 | WIN OR GO HOME

This episode captures the intensity and high stakes faced by the riders as they compete in a crucial event that could make or break their chances of qualifying for XXXXXX. Viewers are taken behind thescenes, witnessing the riders preparation, their mental focus and the bond they share with thwir horses as they enter the arena. The raw emotions and the adrenaline are palpable, creating a sense of excitement for both the riders and the viewers.

04 | FRENEMIES

This episode explores the complex dynamics and rivalries that exist within the showjumping community, focusing on the relationships between riders who are simultaneously friends and competitors. Viewers witness the contrasting emotions that arise when riders face off against their friends-turned-competitors. Frenemies offers an inside look at the behind-the- scenes interactions between riders and the subtle tension that arises when friendship and competition intertwine. The episode showcases the mix of camaraderie and determination as riders strive to outperform each other.

05 | DON'T GET BITTER GET BETTER

In this empowering episode viewers witness the inherent unpredictability of the showjumping world, where even the most skilled riders face obstacles and setbacks. The episode showcases the moments of disappointment, frustration and self-reflection as riders confront their own limitations. This episode highlights how these setbacks serve as catalysts for personal growth and improvement - riders are determined to learn from their mistakes, refine their skills and evolve both as athletes and individuals

06 | CHASING THE DREAM

A compelling and eye opening episode that sheds light on the financial challenges faced by riders in their pursuit of competing at the highest level and the crucial role that sponsorship plays in enabling their dreams. Viewers are introduced to the financial realities of the sport and the significant costs associated with training, horse care, equipment, travel and entry fees. This episode explores the financial strains riders encounter when aiming to compete at the elite level. Throughout the episode, viewers gain insight into the creative strategies riders employ to attract sponsors, the networking required to build relationships and the impact of sponsorship on a rider's ability to access top notch training facilities and high quality horses.

07 | EVERYTHING HAS LEAD TO THIS

In this episode the pressure mounts as Jamie Gornall faces a decisive moment in his quest to represent the UK at the Olympics. This episode showcases the emotional rollercoaster, the heightened nerves and the strategic decisions he must make as he tackles a challenging course.
